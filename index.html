FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?+
We primarily serve Delhi NCR — covering New Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. For premium projects in other cities, we can discuss customised arrangements.
How much does an interior design project cost?+
Project costs vary based on scope, area size, and material choices. Our residential projects typically start from ₹8–12 lakh for a 2BHK. We offer transparent, itemised quotations with no hidden costs after the initial consultation.
How long does a full interior design project take?+
A standard 3BHK home takes 10–14 weeks — including 2–3 weeks of design planning, 6–9 weeks of execution, and 1–2 weeks of finishing and styling. Timelines are discussed and agreed upon before we begin.
Do you provide 3D visualisations before starting work?+
Absolutely. Every project begins with photo-realistic 3D renders and a virtual walkthrough. You approve every detail — furniture, colours, lighting, materials — before a single nail is hammered. Changes are free at this stage.
Can you work with my existing furniture?+
Yes, we can design around your existing pieces — especially heirloom furniture or items with sentimental value. We're skilled at blending old and new beautifully.
What is included in your turnkey service?+
Our turnkey service covers: concept design, 3D renders, material procurement, civil work, electrical and plumbing, carpentry and false ceilings, painting, furniture sourcing, decor and styling, and a final quality walkthrough. You get a ready-to-live-in space.
Do you offer any warranty on the work done?+
Yes. We offer a 1-year service warranty on all execution work and a 5-year warranty on modular kitchen carcasses. Any snags identified within the warranty period are rectified at no extra cost.
How do I get started?+
Simply fill the contact form below, call us at +91 98765 43210, or send us a WhatsApp message. We'll schedule a complimentary 30-minute discovery call within 24 hours to discuss your project.
