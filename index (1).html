Frequently Asked Questions
What is the typical timeline for a residential interior design project? +

The timeline varies depending on the project scope, but typically ranges from 6-12 weeks. This includes design planning (2-3 weeks), execution (3-8 weeks), and final finishing (1-2 weeks). We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

How much does interior design cost? +

Costs depend on the project scope, area size, and design complexity. We offer flexible pricing models including hourly consultations, fixed project fees, and cost-plus arrangements. We can work within various budgets to deliver exceptional results.

Do you provide 3D visualizations before execution? +

Yes, absolutely. We create detailed 3D renderings of the space with all design elements, color schemes, and furniture placement. This helps you visualize the final result and make informed decisions before any work begins.

Can you work within my budget constraints? +

Definitely. We believe great design is achievable at any budget level. During consultation, we discuss your financial parameters and create solutions that maximize impact within your budget. We're transparent about all costs upfront.

Do you handle both design and execution? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive turnkey solutions. Our team manages both the design phase and the complete execution including coordination with contractors, material procurement, quality checks, and final finishing.

How do you incorporate sustainability in design? +

Sustainability is core to our philosophy. We use eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient solutions, and sustainable practices. We focus on creating timeless designs that reduce waste and minimize environmental impact.

What areas do you serve? +

We primarily serve Delhi NCR region including Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Greater Noida, and Faridabad. For projects outside this area, we can discuss customized arrangements based on project requirements.
